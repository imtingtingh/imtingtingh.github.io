City Planning and Project Management (Chinese Version)


Colorful Rural Yulin is a rural complex in Guangxi,China. This is a project I worked with Xiaofei Qin, Haoyu Zhang, Xuanfang Xu for Project Management and Feasibility Analysis at Sun Yat-sen University. We set the development orientation and goals for the site through development conditions assessment, and proposed development strategies and the theme both in industry and space based on the features of the site, we also carried on the feasibility analysis and risk assessment at the end of the report.



General Planning of Huangge Town is a town planning in Nansha, Guangzhou. This is a semester long project I worked with my teammeates (Die Xu, Jiafan Cheng, Qixin Wei, Wei Xiao, Jingluan Yang) at Sun Yat-sen University in Spring 2018. The book contains existing condition analysis and recommendations in land use, transportation connectivity, infrastructure, green space, and landscape. The final chapter dedicates to the environmental resiliency.
