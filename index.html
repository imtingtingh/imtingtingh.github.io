About me

Hello! My name is Tingting Huang, and I hold a master's degree in City Planning with a specialization in Transportation from the University of Pennsylvania. Originally from Guangzhou, China, I have cultivated a diverse academic background, having obtained a Bachelor's Degree in Human Geography & Urban Planning with a minor in Finance from Sun Yat-sen University. Currently, I am working as a software product manager at SF Express in Shenzhen, China.
With a passion for exploring cities, I like using statistical and mapping tools to unravel the intricacies that lie within them. Moreover, I derive great joy from embarking on journeys to new destinations, engaging with individuals from diverse backgrounds, and listening to their unique stories.
I'm a data lover, a culinary art enthusiast, and an amateur photographer. I play piano and I love jazz music. I'm always excited to meet people and share our brilliant ideas with each other.

Fun

Jane's Walk is an annual global event that celebrates the life and ideas of urban activist Jane Jacobs. It involves free, citizen-led walking tours in cities around the world, where people explore their neighborhoods, engage in discussions about local history, architecture, urban design, and community issues. The event was inspired by Jane Jacobs, an influential writer and activist who championed community-driven urban planning and development. She believed that local knowledge and grassroots participation are essential for creating vibrant and livable cities.
